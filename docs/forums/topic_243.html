Palmtree (Wilshire & New Hampshire)- I only go here when there's special event or I'm with a big group. They got rid of the bowling alley and turned it into a club which made it better. It has dance floor, and it has karoke where people can sing. On the weekends, they provide booking just like other clubs which is pretty cool but only bad thing is that they fockin' charge much as clubs. Other than that, it's coo place to kick it at.

Bleu (6th & Alexandria) - I go here when there's sports events. Perfect place to drink your beer or hard liquor while watching the game with your homies. This place is well decorated and has pretty bartenders. One bad thing is that, sometimes, they don't let you smoke inside the cafe so you have to go outside to smoke. I think they will lose lot of customers for that...

I've only been to a few places in Ktown. I've been to Nandarang once, Cafe Bleu once, Pointe a few times...

My favorite place though is Chuh-gah-jeep. 3rd St. between Catalina and I think Berendo. It has this nice "dirty" vibe to it. Like you're at home, or something, or at like a real chuh-gah-jeep. Nice and comfy.

Everytime I go though, I see underage kids. Doesn't bother me, although I do get sick of hearing their dumb stories (they talk really loud sometimes). The girls there aren't really cute. The chairs aren't the most comfy.... The decor ain't that great... The music they play isn't great either...

...but I keep going back. Perhaps it's the nice "homey" feel I get. Perhaps it's because the owners always greet me and offer free food/drinks. Perhaps it's because the valet guy there gives my car priority. Maybe because the waitresses there are nice...

At one point, I was going there at least 3 times/week. It was only about a mile from work, and on the way home, so why not? I'm pretty sure I was their best customer for a period of 3 months or so. Unfortunately, these days I only go once a month, or every 2 months, but I still go, and when I do, I always am treated like family.

let's see...

zip - the outside patio area is nicely decorated with strings of lights hanging overhead. they have live music playing and most of the time it's good, soothing stuff. i've only had lunch there so i'm not sure what the nighttime menu is like but the food is pretty good and the drinks aren't bad either. more than the quality of the food/drinks, the atmosphere is really nice.

blink - the decor is great, service is super but they need some better bartenders up in there. she didn't even know what a grey goose gimlet was! but other than that, it's a nice place to go. always poppin'...on its way to being one of ktown's best hotspots.

pointe - yeah yeah i know i know. everyone hates the place...but how could i hate it when it's like "cheers" to me. well not so much anymore ever since everyone i know quit (wonder why huh?) but i still get hooked up whenever i go. can't beat a night of free drinking, eating and singing.

chin-gu - this place has THE BEST mayo/gochujang dip thing that they serve with cucumbers and carrots. it's so good i go there just to eat that. sometimes i think they get annoyed with me cuz i ask for 10 plates of veggies...but it's good!

nandarang - has decent food but most of all they have a coffee-banana shake that's to die for.

bleu - good food, good crowd, live performances once in a while. good stuff.
