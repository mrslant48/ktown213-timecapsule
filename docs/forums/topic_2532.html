if you speak korean (works if you just speak english but works better if you speak korean), you can just call them and say that you haven't been there in a few years and so your waiter is probably gone. if they ask who your waiter was, just tell him 007, HOT, or some other common korean club waiter name. the guy on the phone will usually assign you a new waiter or will call up one of his waiter friends and have that guy call you.

each table has a minimum. it's around $200 ($140 for a bottle of crown + $40 for the fruit plate). you can get more expensive bottles if you want to.

it's not like century club, white lotus, etc...b/c you have to get a table.

of course like everyone said, there are exceptions to the rules if you know someone pretty well, if you're a hot group of girls, etc.
