Intercrew would be the quietest, but there's no way in hell they could fit your party of 15-20. Bliss, neither because the place is small.

Your best bet would be Blink or Orchid. Blink doesn't do reservations, while Orchid does. You'll have much more room at Orchid, and the food in my opinion is better there.

I haven't been to Blink in ages, but from what I remember, the service was outstanding, while I've heard some complaints about Orchid's service from other people. They have always treated me very well, and I have no complaints with Orchid. I do have one small complaint about Blink, however, and if you think that will make a difference in your decision, you may PM for more details.

Good luck!
