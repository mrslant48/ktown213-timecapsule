6-17-04 - Toro @ The Conga Room

Cyclotron Records teams with Kris B & Pat Schoolastra to bring you a weekly Funky & Progressive House night featuring guests DJs such as Deepsky, John Kelley, Kazell, and others. No cover if you're on the event mailing list. Send an e-mail to chris AT cyclotronrecords dot com to sign up, otherwise, it'll be $5 at the door.

Residents for TORO are: Kris B, Pat Schoolastra, SIM_01, Antonio Arebalo, Jethro, and LRCN.

Located at The Conga Room, 5364 Wilshire Boulevard, L.A. 90036
http://www.congaroom.com

Map
http://www.congaroom.com/Directions.html

9pm - 2am. 21 and over.
