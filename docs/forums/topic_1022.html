RVD... They got almost every game ever invented. It's just like a library of games you can choose from. In the pics where my nuna is picking up the kal-gook-soo, it's right above that place, like on the 2nd floor. In the pics where she's squishing my head w/ the cell phone, that was at an actual Haagen Daaz cafe or something. Like a waitress actually takes your order for ice cream! Anyway, it's right next door to the game bang. So next time you're in Korea, just look for the Haagen-Daaz place, and then look for a "POP CUBE" somewhere on the 2nd floor in that block. It was my nuna's idea to go there, and I didn't want to... but once we started playing, I have to admit, it was probably one of the best times I had while in Korea.

Oh, and the BBQ place, I forget the name, but my cousin said he goes there often, and that its well known, and lots of celebs go there, so you're probably right about the place.

JO3, sure thing... My family is cool, which I'm grateful for. They don't get all pissy that I can't speak Korean very well or sometimes forget some Korean traditions. They understand. My big uncle, aka "The Man"... who I call the soju killer... After meals, he would take me outside and force me to smoke a cigarette. I would resist, but he says he knows what it's like for a smoker after a meal, and told me to just go ahead and smoke.
