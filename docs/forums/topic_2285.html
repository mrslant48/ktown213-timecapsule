The place is incredibly huge, and its interior is eyepopping. The owner is a big fan of scooba diving, and it inspired him to decorate his interior as such. You're surrounded by streaming waterfalls, beautiful lighting, and gives off a very chill vibe. They have a full bar, big screen TV's perfect for Laker games, and check this out. After you get your drink on, take the elevator and go upstairs, because they have 18 rooms of norebang ready to go!!

They've been open for about 1 month, so no one really knows about this great place.

Price is comparable to Bliss and Blink, so y'all need to check it out!!!

6th and Oxford in Ktown.
