It's been a long hard journey, but Ktown213 is finally here! First and foremost, Ktown213 would like to thank everyone that contributed in making this site possible. You know who you are!
Now that Ktown213 has officially launched, we promise to work hard in bringing you the best of K-Town week-in and week-out. From weekly photos of K-Town hot spots, to cars and models, along with the best K-Town directory on the web, we will strive to be the best community site out there!
In the future, you might come across one of our staff members when you're hanging out somewhere in K-Town. They might even ask you to take a picture. And when they do, don't be shy. Just smile, because you're on Ktown213!
Every weekend, our staff goes around snapping away on people having fun in Ktown. We will upload new pictures every week and maintain a full archive. Let's show the world that Ktown213 really knows how to party!
On a scorching day where the temperature was flirting with triple digits, hundreds of Koreans gathered at Wilshire Park Place on Wilshire and Oxford to enjoy the 12th Annual Korean Family Summer Festival. This jam packed event included a dance contest, Teen Korea pageant, and concluded with performances by Uptown and Jinusean. As a Korean American who lived in Ktown all his life, it still brings me joy to see the community come together for an event such as this.

Tony Kim's 2001 Toyota MRS
I'm sure most of you have seen this car on the streets of Ktown and have wondered, "What the hell is that?" This yellow beast is Tony Kim's 2001 Toyota MRS. He has been fixing up cars for years and this badboy was his latest project. This ride has been featured on numerous automobile magazines including Super Street, Max Car UK, Maxi Tuning, and even Playboy. Find out more about Tony and his ride, as we sit with him and ask him some more questions.

Martin Kim was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina on February 27, 1979. Although Martin spent most of his childhood in Argentina, he periodically spent time in Korea as well. At the age of 10, he moved to Philadelphia and completed his secondary education at the Marple Newtown Senior High School in Newtown Square, PA. At 18, he enrolled at the George Washington University in the nation's capitol. After working at an advertising company in Northern Virginia for a year, Martin currently resides in the Los Angeles area, gaining experience in the acting and entertainment industry.
