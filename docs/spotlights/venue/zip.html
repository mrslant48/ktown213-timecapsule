If you’re looking for a place to just relax, sip on a drink and enjoy some good food, have we got a place for you. Located on Wilshire, just a block west of Western, this fusion restaurant /bar has an incredible selection of fusion food and drinks.

QUALITY OF FOOD
From steak to lobster, Zip has all your bases covered. Their fusion style cooking is not only delicious but beautiful to look at. Their AhnJus are finger-lickin’ good and generous in size.

PRICE
Not bad at all. More expensive than your typical Ktown restaurant, but well worth it. The prices aren't unreasonable by any means. Alcoholic beverage prices are comparable to Pointe and Bliss. One bad thing is that they don’t have draft beer or Hite, but they have an extensive selection of cocktails and hard liquor to make up for it.

CLEANLINESS AND ENVIRONMENT
Zip is probably one of few places in Ktown with their own DJ spinning live music all night long. If you enjoy trance and house this is the place for you. The combination of live music and outdoor seating, around a huge bonfire, is perfect for a beautiful summer night.

SERVICE
Can’t complain too much about the service.

OVERALL
It’s definitely a place I see myself going back. Good vibes, good food and good drinks. What else can you ask for? If you and your friends are looking for a place to hang out on the weekends, give Zip a try. Tell them Ktown213 sent you!
