Guerrilla Gamer

With so many giant retailers springing up in every inch of every city, it's nice to come across a mom-and-pop video game store right in the heart of Koreatown.  It's a place where you don't have to stay in long check out lines waiting on a stranger to beep your product, it's a place where gamers of all ages come to hang out and socialize with familiar faces.  Located on the second floor of Assi Market on 8th Street and Olympic, Guerrilla Gamer has quickly become a popular mecca for most local gamers.  Also known for their friendliness and excellent customer service, it's no surprise to find that so many customers keep coming back week after week.  "It's kind of like soul food for your video game senses, we try to make all of our customers feel comfortable,"  says Jerry Yang, proud owner of the store.  Guerrilla Gamer also has a loyal following from Anime fans, and the store carries Manga comic books, Anime DVD's, and Anime figurines.  "Video games and Anime have always been a big part of my life.  It's what I loved as a kid, and it's what I love now."  So the next time you're in Koreatown and need to satisfy your gaming itch, stop by Guerilla Gamer and ask for Jerry.  He'll be more than happy to discuss your favorite game or Anime, and perhaps sell you a game too.
