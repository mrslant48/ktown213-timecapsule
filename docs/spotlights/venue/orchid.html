Orchid Restaurant & Karaoke

Finally, a K-Town venue with a fully stocked bar led by a competent bartender who knows what he's doing. The bartender's name is no other then HeineKen, a martini aficionado, and he will fix you up a drink faster than you can say "Shaken, not stirred."   With future plans to expand their list of martinis to over 100 different kinds from every parts of the country, Orchid is definitely the place to get your alcoholic fix.

Orchid's decor is simply amazing, inspired by the owner's love for the ocean. With overflowing streams of waterfall on every wall and magnificent coral-like columns shooting towards the ceilings, it gives off a very interesting ambiance of relaxation and serenity.

But wait, it's not all that predictable.  You want special-effects?  Their occasional lightning storms controlled by their special lighting equipment, naturally adds excitement to the atmosphere.


Recent recruitment of a new chef who specializes in Pacific Rim cuisine simply adds to the value of their meals, leaving your taste buds asking for more.  Orchids extensive menu selection includes traditional Korean to Hawaiian cuisine, and Ktown213 recommends their skewers of shrimp, beef, and mussels, and their house special donkatsu hamburger steak.  However, if there's one dish you must try above anything else, the staff members of Ktown213 were thoroughly impressed with Orchid's house special, Mahi Mahi with Pesto.  You must try this fish, it's an euphoric experience for your taste buds! 

Dont forget to come in during the weekday afternoons for your Starbucks experience, because they have a wide selection of flavored coffee as well.



Had a taste of Orchid's great selection of food and drink, and wondering what to do next? Leave your car validated and ride the elevator to the second floor, where 18 rooms of spacious karaoke rooms await you (150 capacity). Orchid has the latest songs and state-of-the-art equipment to suit your norebang needs!
