Q & A with Sally Yi

KT213:  For the dying men in K-Town, what are your stats!
SY: I'm 5'1", 103 lbs, 34B-25-35, and I'm size 1-3.

KT213:  Are you single? What do you look for in a man?
SY:  Yes, I'm single. I have been single for a quite some time now, ha ha. I love a guy with humor, being able to make me laugh will mean the world especially when I am down.
