Ktown213:  Kay, it's all you. Give us your 30-Second Intro!

KT213:  What made you want to become a model, and how did you get your foot in the door? Tell us about "Kay: The Early Years".

I accepted the fact that I'd be working in a corporate office somewhere as my career, but a photographer suggested I get into modeling. He even gave me an "evaluation" of my face. So since I've been a big fan of Playboy, and they have some oh-so beautiful pictures and models, I gave it a shot and here I am today! It took off as soon as it started which was about 9 months ago!

KT213:  Less than a year?! Congrats on that!

KT213:  Now, before we get to the really fun questions, let's do the more informative ones. What are your favorite accomplishments as a model?

I've gotten on two magazine covers within 3 months of modeling and I've been to beautiful places like Tahiti, Jamaica and Hawaii.

And of course, my website www.KaySivilay.com!
