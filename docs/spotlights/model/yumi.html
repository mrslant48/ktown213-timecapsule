Q&A: Playboy Sensation Yumi Lee
Part I: The Interview

Ktown213:  Hi Yumi, first we thank you for doing this interview with Ktown213. Give our viewers your 30-second intro!
Yumi Lee:  Hello, I'm Yumi Lee. You may know me from Playboy TV, Perfect 10 magazine, or numerous other publications. I am currently the Cyber Girl of the Week on Playboy.com so please go check it out! Thanks to all my wonderful fans for your continued loving support!

KT213:  How old were you when you moved to the States, and was it a difficult transition to make?
YL:  I move to the States when I was 13 years old from Seoul. It was a difficult first year because of the sudden changes of language and environment. It was a huge culture shock for me. The adjustment became much easier as the years went by. Now I'm definitely an American!

KT213:  We understand you love sports, even joined the track team in high school! Tell us more about that.
YL:  My whole family are big sports fans. My family encouraged me to join the track team to boost my confidence. I really enjoyed practicing after school and just competing. Now, I can't even come close to the time that I used to run the mile. I am not in that kind of shape anymore. Too much time sitting on my tushy!

KT213:  We know you're a woman who values the importance of education, and you haven't let your modeling career steer you away from that. Kudos to you!
YL:  You can't model forever but learning is a lifelong process. I was raised to value education. Education allows a person to explore their inner self, gain intellect, and pursue their dreams. I know models out there that have no intention of continuing their education but would rather find a rich man to take care of them...that's not my style.

KT213:  You're currently studying Electrical Engineering, how's that going for you?
YL:  I found that quite boring even though I was almost done with the program. I realized I want to have a fulfilling and meaningful career rather than being stuck in the office all day. I'm currently shifting gears towards something in the medical field where I can reach
out to people who are sick and needy.

KT213:  What kind of food do you like? Do you have to constantly watch what you eat to keep your beautiful figure?
YL:  I'm a sucker for food. I love food!!! I pretty much eat everything. My favorites are Korean & Japanese cuisine, a big juicy steak and anything with vegetables. I do follow this one routine though...eat less but more often. That is perhaps the most important habit to follow in order for me to stay in shape.

KT213:  Wow, a Korean woman who is smart, sexy, beautiful, loves galbi, and loves sports! You're the perfect woman for all man-kind!
YL:  Yes, indeed! That's right, just give me a plate of galbi and a good b-ball game!

KT213:  Now for the million dollar follow up question, are you single?
YL:  Yes, I am but I'm still waiting for the Man of my dreams!

KT213:  Have you ever been to K-Town here in Los Angeles? What are some of your favorite places to visit?
YL:  Yes, I've been to K-Town several times. In fact, it's one of my favorite places to visit whenever I'm in LA. I usually go where the food is...hahahahaha! There are so many more varieties of Korean food in K-Town than in Arizona. Oh, I also pick up some Korean novels or biographies or sometimes I'll look for new Korean movies to buy and bring back home with me.

KT213:  Anything you'd like to add?
YL:  I'm the Cyber Girl of the Week Starting Nov. 1st so please check me out at playboy.com. I'm chatting with the fans through the message boards and it's been a blast already. The voting will start in early Dec. for Cyber Girl of the Month and I need your votes!!! I'll be the 1st Korean Cyber Girl of the Month if I get the most votes! I need all the Korean support I can get! Also, don't forget to visit my official website www.YumiLee.com.

KT213:  Well we thank you for the interview Yumi, and on behalf of Ktown213 we wish you the best of luck!
YL:  I want to thank you Ktown213 for the interview and for featuring me on the site!  I love you all and thank you so much!
