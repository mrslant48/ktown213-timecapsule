Q & A with Grace Lee

Ktown213:  Hi Grace, like we ask all our models, give us your 30 second intro.
Grace Lee:  I am currently modeling in the import industry. An aspiring model is what you can say I am. I hope to become a spokesmodel one day for a major company. I am also a go-go dancer for various clubs. As of now I am working fulltime at a mortgage company as an administrative assistant and also attending real estate school. So as you can see, I am a very busy woman!

Kt213:  How long have you been modeling, and how do you like it so far?
GL:  I have been modeling for half a year now...still a newbie model I guess...and I am totally having fun with it...Its something really new for me so its exciting you can say. I made so many great new friends and the best part of all this is that...I also formed a really cool fan base. I never came to think I would have fans ever!

Kt213:  You're fairly new to the modeling industry, are you a little fearful of what's to come? Do you have any regrets?
GL:  I have no regrets in modeling right now...I came into this industry highly acknowledging the pros and cons of modeling. Because I am in the import industry (car shows), I know I will run into a lot of drama and negativity from certain people. What can you say? I am exposing myself as well as my life to the public and by doing that I know some of the consequences that go along with it. I can't make everyone happy and I can't make everyone like me.

Kt213:  What are your recent projects, or upcoming projects to look forward to?
GL:  Right now I don't have any major projects coming up, but I will be working towards becoming a spokesmodel one day. For now I am just trying to expose myself more the the people, have them know who I am and what I am about. I am just doing car shows at the moment.

Kt213:  What are your measurements?
GL:  34B-26-36

Kt213:  Any hobbies?
GL:  I don't really have hobbies.. I mean I guess I can say modeling right now would be a hobby of mine! GO-GO dancing would be a hobby as well for me.. I'm so busy with working full time and also attending night classes that I don't really go out too much.

Kt213:  What are your turn-on's and turn-off's in a guy?
GL:  Turn on's: Someone who definitely has a cute smile, I like guys who always smell good, I love guys who makes me laugh, someone who is really kick back and down for whatever, and always looking good!
Turn offs: Someone who has a short temper, bad breath, someone who is uptight, anyone who is just plain boring!  Basically, a guy who can put up with me, have patience, and hang with me is someone that can possibly date me. Ha hah.

Kt213:  Are you single? How often do you date?

GL:  I am dating someone right now...it isn't official though. One can say that I am boy crazy. I love to meet new guys and kick it with them. I don't really date to often though (not by choice). Ha ha. Its really hard to date people and carry on a relationship if you are in this industry. As far as meeting guys to date...its really hard to determine whether they like you for who you are.

Kt213:  Where do you see yourself 5 years from now?
GL:  I see myself getting married hopefully in 5 years and just taking care of my family. By then I plan to completely retire from modeling and just carry on a normal life.

Kt213:  Thank you for taking the time doing this interview, and on behalf of Ktown213.com I hope big things happen for you and we wish you best of luck!
GL:  Thank you Ktown213.com!
