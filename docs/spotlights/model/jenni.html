Q & A with Jenni Choi

Ktown213:  Jenni, not a lot of people know about you in Southern California, please give us a quick intro.
Jenni Choi:  Hey everyone!  My name is Jenni Choi!  I was born in Korea, and when I was about eight years old I moved to Maryland with my parents and my older brother.  Currently I'm a college student majoring in pharmaceuticals but I'm thinking about going into nursing as well.

KT213:  Jenni, tell us how you got your start in import modeling.
JC:  Well, I got started first when I was in the 10th grade.  I did a little bit of modeling for clothes and ads before, but I started modeling for import cars not that long ago.  Maybe about 2 years ago for a friend's car. After that I started modeling for teams around the east coast.

KT213:  That's great, you must have a lot of fun doing that.  What do you do on your spare time?
JC:  On my spare time i like to shop and hang out with my friends.

KT213:  What are your turn-on's in a guy?
JC:  A man who is smart, and someone I can have a conversation with about anything. Also he has to have patience.  A guy with a humor is a plus, because I need a guy that can cheer me up when I'm feeling down. A guy with motivation and goals.

 KT213:  Hear that guys?  Please take notes!  Now Jenni, where can we see more of you? 
JC:  Well this year you won't be seeing me much, but next year I will be attending more shows so please check me out!

KT213:  And this is a question I ask all our fine ladies Jenni, state your measurements!
JC:  Ha ha, I'm 34-26-36

 KT213:  Well obviously I can't ask you what your favorite things are about K-Town, so what do you like about our site?
JC:  Everything!  I'm so glad there's a website like this, and I'm honored to be featured on such a great site!

 KT213:  Thanks Jenni, and we hope you have a wonderful time doing what you do! 
JC:  Thank you!  Mad love to K-Town! *muah*
