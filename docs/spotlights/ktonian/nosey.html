Mr. & Ms. KTown?  What the heck is that?  Well simply put, this is a page put up to feature a regular Joe/Jane of K-town, LA.  Every week, we try to gather a familiar face from the community, giving them the opportunity to shine!

Q & A with Nosey...

Q:  What do you do?
A:  I'm currently a student at USC preparing myself for med school.  I'm in my last year and I can't wait 'til graduation!

Q:  Where do you like to hang out in K-town?
A:  Where ever my friends go, but preferably where some fine ladies hang out.  I'm not being shallow am I?

Q:  Any pros and cons of hanging out in K-town?
A:  Well the good thing about K-Town is that there are tons of place to hang out.  It seems like the whole town is designed specifically to cater to young Korean-Americans.  The bad part of K-Town is that it's become somewhat violent on certain nights.  I think lots of Korean guys have this chip on their shoulder, and they're looking for trouble and they ruin the fun for many good people in K-Town.

Q:  How did you get the nickname "Nosey?"
A:  C'mon now, it doesn't take a genius to figure that one out.

Q:  What do you like to do in your spare time?
A:  Many things...but mostly I like to work on my Audi A4, go mountain biking, and soak up the sun at the beach.

Q:  What are your turn-ons and turn-offs in a girl?
A:  I hate materialistic girls, but living in K-Town that's kind of like an oxymoron isn't it?  Hehe j/k girls!  Seriously, there are some good girls in K-Town.  But I really like girls that know how to carry themselves, because that's sexy.

Q:  How the heck did you get to be featured as the first Mr. K-Town of the week?
A:  Well since I know you guys, the creative directors of Ktown213, y'all hooked it up!
