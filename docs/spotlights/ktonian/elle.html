Elle Hamm
Elle Hamm, consummate host, performer, artist, and personality. Her professional lineage boasts a unique mix of cutting-edge roles: entrepreneur/CEO (Rudwear Collection), television host/personality (Source: All Access-Syndicated, Farmclub.com, The X-Show, Chinese Take-Out), and designer/consultant (WNBA, UCLA, Oakland Raiders). Shes an unstoppable force in inspiring and motivating the coveted young, urban demographic.
Now a well-seasoned entertainment personality, she assumes a major role as the Hostess for Half Baked. After an extensive search for the perfect host, National Lampoon Network selected Elle Hamm to host its celebrity cooking show. This new program highlights the hometowns and personal food creations of well-known public figures, such as Shaquille ONeal and Tara Reid, and renowned chefs from some of the countrys hottest eateries. Ms. Hamm, full of international flavor and refreshing candor, provides the punch necessary to spice up the show.

Not content to spend her time wining and dining on celebrity cuisine, Elles visage will be featured in print and on television. Catch her exotic looks on the cover of Nikes Fall 2002 Catalog and in the National ACLU Ads. Her commercials for Verizon, LA Looks, Netzero.net, JC Penney, and Sprite will cement her reputation as a multi-media force to be reckoned with Her mass appeal demonstrates itself in clothing, entertainment, food, and drink.

Still an avid participant in behind-the-scenes operations, Elles opinion commands respect from her peers. As the West Coast Editor for her own monthly column, ELLE-ments of Fashion & Beauty, kicking off in May 2003, Elle will offer her thoughts and expert advice to the readership magazine, Black Hairstyles and Trends.

Despite massive growth in her entertainment career, Elle remains loyal to her fashion and promotional merchandise design roots. Q&A with Elle Hamm

Ktown213:  First thank you for taking the time for the interview. Elle, please give our viewers your 30 second intro!
Elle Hamm:  Hey, it's Elle Hamm and there's a whole lot of changes!  Read on and find out what's been going on in your Korean sista, Elle Hamm's world!  Welcome!

KT213:  That's quite an intro, ha ha...To sum it all up, you're a host, personality, performer, designer, artist, entrepreneur, and a successful business woman.  My lord, I almost ran out of breath!  How do you do it?  Where do you find the time?
EH:  A great team of people and lot's of time management!  That's the key!

KT213:  You have remained loyal to your fashion and promotional
merchandise design roots.  Tell our readers more about Rudwear.
EH:  Definitely!  The private label and merchandising side of things have been very busy!  Elle Hamm & Co. have been making clothing for the skateboard industry for skate brands such as Chocolate, Diamond Supply Co., Fillmore Wheels, GIRL, and etc.  So when you readers need some T-shirts made for an event, you know who to call!  As far as Rudwear we have desolved the clothing co., but started a sick new line of washed, fashion forward T-shirts call Culture Factor!  So check that out at www.culturefactor.com for more info.

KT213:  As I mentioned above, you're a multi-talented individual. Which aspects of your career do you enjoy the most?
EH: I enjoy more of the creative side of things...writing, singing and coming up with concepts for songs and designs, and etc.


KT213:  What's next for Elle Hamm?
EH: Elle Hamm's music and Culture Factor... All the way to the top!

KT213:  What do you love about K-Town? What are some of your favorite places to hang out?
EH: I love my Koreans! As far as my favorite hang outs go, Sunset Plaza, Olympic Spa, the recording studio or my parents house, just chillin!

KT213:  I have to ask you on behalf of all breathing men in Ktown, are you single?
EH: Yes!

KT213:  What are some of your turn-ON's and turnoff's?
EH:  My turn-on's are God fearing men. My turn-off's are men with bad teeth!  Ha ha ha!

KT213:  Well, it's been fun.  We thank you for your time Elle, and we wish you the best of luck with everything you do!
EH: Thank you ktown213, it's always a pleasure to chill and hang out with my peoples!
