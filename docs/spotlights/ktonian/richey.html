Richey Lam
Ktown213: Hey Richey give our viewers your 30 sec intro!
Richey Lam: Pure unadulterated wholesome fun! Is how I like to describe myself, Something for everybody. My shows, for the most part are PG rated with a couple of Bleeps now and then. I do encourage everybody to read my disclaimer on my website www.richeylam.com before every show! Ha!

Kt213: Give us a little background, where did you grow up and what made you fall in love with music and performing?
RL: I grew up in a small suburb of Alpharetta, GA. Ive been surrounded by music my whole life. Whether it be from church or choir or my grandfather, who was the Hong Kong symphony conductor for 25 years. Ive been performing ever since I can remember. I use to love the original Star Search with Ed McMahon. I always wanted to be a contestant, I thought I would have given those kids a run for their money.

Kt213: Being from the south, Whats that like?
RL: Awesome! I love the south. A lot of my style comes from southern roots. I definitely was the only Asian growing up. Theres a lot of stereotypes you might think of when you think of GA. And a lot of them are true, but theres only good thoughts that come to my mind. Its where Im from and Im proud to represent.

Kt213: I personally saw you perform at PKs Kollaboration last year and you simply rocked the house. Were you happy with your performance there?
RL: Definitely! I couldnt have asked for a better and more supportive audience. Id love to come back and perform.

Kt213: Where can we see more of Richey Lam? Any upcoming projects?
RL: I actually just played a show at The Mint in Hollywood. Im always doing shows all over LA. Im currently booking shows up the west coast, playing as many colleges that will let me.

Kt213: For all the ladies in K-Town, are you single? What are your turn-ons and turn-offs?
RL: Single and free as a bird! The kind of girls I like would have to be down to earth, All Na.Tu.RAL! A girl who could laugh and cut up with me. Someone who is sweet, but no too sweet when were alone.
Turn ons- Sundresses, Glasses, Peach Fuzz, Straight teeth, Home made cooking, Pastel colors, white pants, Skirts, Pony tails, Athletic, funny, witty, good dancer, Good singer, Cool.
Turn offs- Bad Breath and Grandma panties!

Kt213: Ha ha ha, grandma panties...sorry.  Whats your impression of K-Town so far? Any favorite hang-outs?
RL: I love K-Town. Not too familiar with the place, but willing to find out.

Kt213: Well we thank you for your time Richey, and we wish you the best of luck!
RL: Thanks K-Town!
