Mr. & Ms. KTown?  What the heck is that?  Well simply put, this is a page put up to feature a regular Joe/Jane of K-town, LA.  Every week, we try to gather a familiar face from the community, giving them the opportunity to shine!

 

Martin Eduardo Kim was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina on February 27, 1979. Although Martin spent most of his childhood in Argentina, he periodically spent time in Korea as well. At the age of 10, he moved to Philadelphia and completed his secondary education at the Marple Newtown Senior High School in Newtown Square, PA. At 18, he enrolled at the George Washington University in the nation's capitol. After working at an advertising company in Northern Virginia for a year, Martin currently resides in the Los Angeles area, gaining experience in the acting and entertainment industry.

At a young age, Martin's desire to entertain has always been present. He has regularly been on stage singing, performing, playing instruments, and even emceeing since the age of eight. Realizing the importance of higher education, Martin pursued a bachelor's degree at The George Washington University. During college, Martin learned the most important lesson of his life: One must follow his passion. This has motivated him to aspire after what he has always known was meant for him: the dream to become an entertainer. Since his bold move to Los Angeles, he has appeared in several independent films as well as a roll in the Korean drama, "All In."

Anything can be accomplished when he sets his heart and mind to it. His passion and dedication for his future is a promise that he will achieve this dream. Martin has been gifted with an admirable personality and natural talent. He is now prepared to share that gift with whoever is willing to give him the opportunity.
