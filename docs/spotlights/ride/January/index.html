1999 LEXUS GS300
Part I: The Interview

Ktown213:  Hey Stef, tell us about your team, your ride, and yourself, and how you got into modifying your car.
Stef Oshima: Cars have always been a hobby of mine every since I was a kid. I think a few years ago I really got into knowing what was out there and did a lot of research on cars and which mods are good and efficient. I had a 2001 Civic Coupe before this car and was going to swap the motor with a K20A from Japan. After thinking about it I figured that with that kind of investment I could go for a different project.

As soon as I got the GS300 I was going to just enjoy it and drive it around stock for at least some months. Yeah right, like that was going to happen, haha... The next few days I threw on rims, wing, front bumper, roof spoiler, and little things here and there. From that point on 'till now is history... My car, or "The Beast" as people like to refer it as, runs about 460hp to the wheels and about 500hp to the crank. It's a Single Custom Turbo Kit from Spark Racing. I'm boosting about 18psi and had all the major upgrades to the motor. The look I guess that I'm trying to keep it as is just clean but aggressive. Gotta say thanks to SPARK RACING (Joey, Anthony, Kevin, and Jerry) who made it possible to make something that doesn't really seem possible. Can't forget THANKS to Jay & Hiro from ABFLUG USA my other two bosses!!!! So many nights and so many ideas.. Too bad there's more for you guys to work out for me. Thanks for the (2) new tires Jay!! haha..

The company I'm in is called ABFLUG USA and for now specialize in Lexus, Supra, and RX-7.. It's a company based in Japan and our friend decided to open the market in the US. So in the company is 2 guys, and myself but recently people when they talk about us refer us as Team ABFLUG.. Haha.. Whatever works is cool with me. Since then we have some friends with pretty hooked up cars that is in our "Team" so to speak which is cool. We entered a few shows, just to let the people know what we carry and what's available for them.

KT213:  What are your future plans for your car?
SO:  Future plans for my car?? Hmm.. Everyone tells me that I'm practically done with my car but there's never enough room for perfection. There's still a lot more ideas and mod's that are going to be slapped on this beast but until then we'll have to see...

KT213:  What's your favorite joint in Ktown?
SO:  There's soooo many places now in Ktown that it's hard to keep up. Places that I just like going to for like K-Club would be places like VR or Le Prive. I heard that there are other places but haven't had a chance to go check them out yet. Nandarang is cool b/c it gives you the outside patio effect, Green Belt, NY NY, and ol'school Intercrew which has been there since I was underage to drink!!

KT213:  Awesome, bro! Hey, thanks a lot for taking the time for this interview! Make sure to keep us posted with updates on your car!
SO :  Thanks, Ktown213! I'll definitely keep you guys posted! See you guys in Ktown!
