Bryan Choi was born in Brooklyn, New York in 1976, and currently resides in Highland, California.  In the past he has worked in the areas of graphic design, illustration, and marketing, and is now currently studying to be a teacher.
The following comic strips *Poor Poor Asian Guy and College Boys are some of Bryan's old work, but he is currently working on another project based on
his personal life in efforts to syndicate his new work through newspapers.

Meet the Characters from "College Boys"© at University Parkway Avenue

Fred
Major: UndecidedA regular joe college student, Fred always seems to have a short temper along with his lingering pessimism. But he does have a soft side, he just has a hard time showing it. He has a thing for cars and Jo-Jo.
 
Roger
Major: Computer Science
Fred's roommate and friend. His personality is different from Fred which is why they get along. He is adventurous and spontaneous. He has a thing for Fred's step sister, Peggy.
 
Stacey
Major: Psychology
One of Fred's best friends. Being a psychologist in training, she's always willing to listen to his problems. She is kind and cheerful which is why Fred enjoys her company. She has a dog named Buster and works at a coffee shop.

Peggy
Major: Business
Sweet, cute with an irresistable personality makes Peggy the most sought after by most guys. Her dominating father is a commander in the military who made Fred the reluctant guardian for her safe keeping and keeping guys away from her. She loves the college scene and hangs out at the coffee shop.

Jo-Jo
Major: Engineering
Jo-Jo short for Joanne met Fred during a road rage where she punched his lights out. This vicious tomboy takes no crap from any guy and she has the power to back it up. Despite her tough exterior, she has a nice sensitive side. She works at Burger Hut and on her off time her interests include cars, sports and travel.
 
Beast Boy
Major: ???
His whereabouts is unknown, Fred just calls him Beast Boy. He rescued Stacey and Peggy when they were about to be robbed. Although Fred doesn't like the savage kid, Beast Boy enjoys being around Fred. He displays unbelievable strength, agility and nimbleness. Peggy seems to feel for the tyke. Although he can't talk and his past is cloudy, Beast Boy has found a new group of friends that he has been loyal to.

Scalper Willy
Major: Undecided
Always scrounging for money and coming up with schemes to rip people off, Scalper Willy cares for no body but himself. But he does have a social life along side his frat brothers. Known to be a party animal and doing outrageous things just for the sheer thrill of it all.

Max
Major: Business
As the rich pampered preppy and the president of the Alpha Beta Sigma fraternity, Max believes he is the superior than everyone else and will do anything to prove it. He likes to give "commoners" a hard time, especially Fred and Roger. However he does have a thing for Peggy which some what complicates things.

Nate
Major: Law
An all round suave good guy. He also is a reluctant member of the Alpha Beta Sigmas, however he is really cool, but modest about it. A real ladies' man and he seems to be good at EVERYTHING. Fred is skeptical and jealous of him, even though he knows Nate is a nice guy. He is mysterious as if he leads a secret life...
