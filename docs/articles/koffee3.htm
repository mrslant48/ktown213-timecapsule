New Korean name for condom too close for comfort

Plans to cease using the English word condom in favor of a more Korean term have been set aside.  The Korean Anti-AIDS Foundation held a contest to rename the rubber prophylactic and came up with ae-pil instead of the commonly used word condom.  Complaints due to the similarity of ae-pil and some surnames in Korea have halted plans to implement the use of the new term.  Does anybody but me find it disturbing that reportedly only 10 percent of people in Korea use condoms when having sex??
