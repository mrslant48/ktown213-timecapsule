Asian Films Compete at Cannes
“Eight local films will be among the Asian films heading to the 58th Cannes Film Festival, taking part in competition and non-competition categories from May 11-22, and presents 53 films from 28 countries.”  Kim Di-duk’s “The Bow,”  Ryu Seung-wan’s “Crying Fist,” and  Lim Sang-soo’s “Tale of Cinema” will compete for South Korea’s along with a few other Korean Films.
