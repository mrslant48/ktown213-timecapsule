China Foils News Conference by South Korean Lawmakers
The news conference of four South Korean legislators was brought to an abrupt halt with the lights and microphones suddenly turned off in Beijing recently. Though the men who broke up the news conference refused to identify themselves, the demeanor in which the conference was disbanded bore the rough trademark of the Chinese government. In addition, the unidentified muscle demanded that the lawmakers from South Korea seek approval from China's Foreign Ministry for such an event as the one that was in progress. I'm sorry, no offense to Chinese people because you guys are cool, but  screw your government.
