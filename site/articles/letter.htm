Letter from the Editor
BY LIZ FEOLA

Letter from the editor...
Welcome to the articles page. My highly trained and grossly underpaid elves are behind the scenes working hard to bring you fresh and relevant new articles. The team of elves that was previously in charge of the article page were found one morning passed out and naked after a wild night of sniffing paint thinner and blowing several thousand dollars gambling on the all-east senior division speed-crocheting tournament. Needless to say, that merry band of elves have since been relieved of their positions at ktown213 for dereliction of duty and asked politely to never, under any circumstances, return to Money's basement.

If you find that you have become a slave to your muse and suddenly have the urge to sit down and type out a couple hundred words, submissions from members of the community are not only welcome, but encouraged. Tell us what's on your mind, review the soul-food restaurants in your neighborhood, tell us about your last trip to the Grand Canyon, or the thrill of your first adventure paragliding. We're ready for your jelly.  Please submit your articles to lfeola@ktown213.com.
